FAQ

Is my data secure?
Yes. Private deployment options ensure your data never leaves your environment. No public AI models are used.
Does this replace patent attorneys?
No. The copilot drafts first versions; attorneys review, refine, and approve. You stay in control.
Do we need to change our workflow?
Minimal change. You paste or upload material as you do today; the copilot produces a structured draft for you to refine.
How long does deployment take?
Depends on your environment and training data. As a design partner, we work with you to define timelines.
